About Task Track

Task Track is your personal task manager that helps you keep track of your tasks and deadlines.

Features

  • Add Tasks - Create new tasks with text, priority, deadline, and category
  • Priority Levels - Mark tasks as Low, Medium, or High priority
  • Categories - Organize tasks into Private, Work, or School categories
  • Deadlines - Set date and time for when tasks should be completed
  • Sorting - Sort tasks by ID, priority, or category
  • Notifications - Get warnings when a task has less than 1 day or 1 hour remaining
  • Status - Mark tasks as complete with a checkbox

How to Use Task Track?

  1. Enter your task in the "New Task" field
  2. Select priority, deadline, and category (optional)
  3. Click "Add" to add the task
  4. Check off tasks when they are complete
  5. Delete tasks by clicking the "X" button

Color Coding

  • White glow - Less than 1 day remaining until deadline
  • Red glow - Less than 1 hour remaining until deadline
  • (Time is up!) - The deadline has passed
